Privacy policy

We don't collect your data.

QuranDesk is built for private listening — no accounts, no ads, no analytics, no tracking.

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Information We Collect

QuranDesk does not collect personal information from you. We do not ask you to create an account, sign in, provide your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, microphone input, or payment details.

Information Stored On Your Device

QuranDesk keeps things on your device so it works offline and remembers where you left off — your downloaded audio, the list of reciters, your playback spot, and your settings.

This local data remains on your device unless you delete it in the app, uninstall QuranDesk, clear app data through your operating system, or back up/export your device through operating-system features outside QuranDesk.

Network Requests

QuranDesk uses the internet only to run and stay up to date: to load the list of reciters and their images, download the audio you ask for, and check for app updates.

That's it. No analytics, advertising, or tracking is ever added to these requests.

Sharing And Selling Data

QuranDesk does not sell, rent, or share personal data because the app does not collect personal data.

Children's Privacy

QuranDesk is a general-audience recitation app. It does not collect personal information from children or adults.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email support@qurandesk.app. QuranDesk is made by Ebee Solutions.
