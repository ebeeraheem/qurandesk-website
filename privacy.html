Privacy policy

QuranDesk does not collect personal data.

QuranDesk is built for private listening. The app does not use accounts, analytics, ads, tracking, or third-party personal-data collection. It is developed by Ebee Solutions.

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Information We Collect

QuranDesk does not collect personal information from you. We do not ask you to create an account, sign in, provide your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, microphone input, or payment details.

Information Stored On Your Device

QuranDesk stores app data locally so the app can work offline and remember your listening state. This can include downloaded audio files, cached reciter catalog data, download status, playback position, settings, and theme preferences.

This local data remains on your device unless you delete it in the app, uninstall QuranDesk, clear app data through your operating system, or back up/export your device through operating-system features outside QuranDesk.

Network Requests

QuranDesk uses the internet only for the app to function and update: it fetches the public reciter catalog, reciter images, audio files, desktop release metadata, and mobile app updates or store-delivered updates.

These requests are made to the services that host QuranDesk content and app updates. QuranDesk does not add analytics, advertising, or tracking code to these requests.

Sharing And Selling Data

QuranDesk does not sell, rent, or share personal data because the app does not collect personal data.

Children's Privacy

QuranDesk is a general-audience recitation app. It does not collect personal information from children or adults.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, Ebee Solutions, or QuranDesk support, email support@qurandesk.app.
