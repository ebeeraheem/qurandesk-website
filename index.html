Details

Questions people usually ask first

Does QuranDesk work without internet?

Yes. You need internet to refresh the catalog and download recitations. After that, downloaded surahs play from your computer.

Where are downloads stored?

QuranDesk keeps audio files in its application data folder and includes a settings shortcut to reveal that folder in your file explorer.

Who builds QuranDesk?

QuranDesk is built by Ebee Solutions, a technology studio in Kano, Nigeria creating useful digital products for local and global communities.
